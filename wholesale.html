    Wholesale & Custom Orders

    If you’ve been searching for a fresh, new marketing strategy or a great party favor, look no further! Pure Joy Body can create custom, personalized designs and labels exclusively for your small business or event. Hannah will work with you one on one to make sure that you are not only getting a product that people will be raving about, but also a design that’s right for you!

    Our Process

    learn more

  • First, you’ll fill out our questionnaire. This will help us become more familiar with the event you are hosting or the business you represent. It will also give us a general idea of what you are looking for in a design.
  • Upon receipt, Hannah will then contact you via e-mail to discuss your vision in more detail.
  • Prior to printing, you will receive proofs of the finished design for review.
  • We charge a personalization fee of $15.00 per design. A 50% non-refundable deposit is required upon placing your custom order, with the balance of your payment due upon approval of the design proof.
